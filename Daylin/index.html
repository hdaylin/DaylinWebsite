One of my greatest accomplishments Emerging Coders

An empathetic way to learn code.

After gaining a deep understanding of how underrepresented women and minorities are in the tech industry and also reflecting on my personal struggles with learning technical skills I decided to create Emerging Coders at Howard University. I wanted us to have a safe space for students to learn how to code, be creative, share ideas, and also network with those who have a similar passion.

One of the greatest gifts from Emerging Coders is the community within our department that has been built. We've spent long nights together honing our crafts and shared the things we've learned. It’s amazing to see Emerging Coders alumni now working for huge companies such as Facebook, Adobe, Deloitte, Lockheed Martin, and Accenture to name a few.
